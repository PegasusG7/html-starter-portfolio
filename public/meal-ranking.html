Johnny's Favorite Meals

My Top 3 Meals

(Because choosing movies was too hard)

Cereal

There are so many delicious cereals, but the best is Milo cereal. It's amazingly tasty and crunchy, and a treat to eat!

Pizza

Pizza is a classic for a reason. The best pizza is margherita pizza - bread, cheese and tomato; you don't need anything more than that.

Spaghetti Bolognese

When I was at school, my mom made me spaghetti bolognese every Monday; it helped me so much to get through the school day!
